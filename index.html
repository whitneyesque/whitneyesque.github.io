What I Do

Service design is what happens when you zoom out past the screen.

How does this whole system work? Who's involved? Where do people get stuck? What's the business model underneath? I answer those questions, then work across teams to turn the answers into services that actually function for the people using them.

That means research with real users, workshops with cross-functional teams, journey maps and blueprints that make complexity visible, and prototypes that test whether the thing we're building will survive contact with reality.
